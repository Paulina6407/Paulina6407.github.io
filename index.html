About Me

Passionate about the science of communication

I'm Paulina — a first-year student at William Paterson University in New Jersey, pursuing a degree in Speech-Language Pathology. I'm driven by curiosity about how humans communicate and a deep desire to help those who struggle to be heard.

For as long as I can remember, I've been drawn to working with young children. I genuinely love meeting new kids — their energy, their curiosity, and the way they see the world. I've always had a natural patience with them, which I believe is one of the most important qualities a pediatric SLP can have.

My dream is to work as a Speech-Language Pathologist in a pediatric hospital setting — supporting children facing communication challenges due to illness, injury, or developmental conditions, and being part of a multidisciplinary care team that changes young lives.

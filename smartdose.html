The Problem

Alarm fatigue is real.

When the same alarm fires every day at the same time, people stop hearing it. Smart Dose solves this by making reminders adaptive — not repetitive.

😴
Alarm Fatigue
Patients tune out static reminders over time. A fixed 8 AM alarm means nothing if you're always driving at 8 AM and can't act on it.
💊
Missed Doses
Skipped medications can lead to serious health consequences — especially for heart conditions, diabetes, and chronic illness management.
📍
Context is Ignored
Traditional apps don't know where you are or what you're doing. Smart Dose uses GPS and activity data to find a better moment.
🔕
Alarm Dismissal
Poorly timed alerts get snoozed and eventually turned off entirely, defeating the purpose of the reminder system altogether.
Workflow Design

From data to the perfect moment.

Smart Dose follows a machine learning pipeline — collecting behavioral data, training on patterns, and deploying personalized alerts on your phone.

1
Data Collection
5,000 labeled examples: preferred reminder time, GPS/movement data, and whether the pill was actually taken or the alarm was dismissed.
2
ML Training
The model learns behavioral patterns — like consistently driving at 8 AM — and adjusts to wait for a moment when action is possible.
3
Prediction
For each reminder window, the AI predicts the "perfect moment" — the time when you're most likely to actually take your medication.
4
Deployment
A smartphone app delivers smart notifications — adapting continuously as your routine evolves over time.
AI Input → Output

The intelligence behind the alert.

Smart Dose maps context-rich inputs to a single optimized output — the best moment to remind you.

Input (A)
User Context
  • Preferred reminder time (e.g. 8:00 AM)
  • Current activity (e.g. Driving)
  • GPS location data
  • Historical dismissal patterns
  • Phone movement / accelerometer
🧠
ML Model
Output (B)
Perfect Moment

Technical & Business Due Diligence

Built with care and ethics.

Smart Dose is designed to be helpful, safe, and responsible — especially for life-critical medications.

✅
Technical Feasibility
A nurse or family member could already do this manually. Smart Dose automates and scales that human behavior — making it feasible and proven in concept.
📉
Why 100% Isn't Possible
If a user's phone dies or is left behind, the AI cannot reach them. Hardware dependency is a known limitation. A backup caregiver notification system could help.
💚
Business & Social Value
Poorly timed alarms cause people to disable them permanently. Smart Dose keeps users engaged and safe by respecting their routine — not fighting it.
🚨
Ethics: Emergency Override
For life-critical medications like heart pills, if a dose hasn't been taken within 30 minutes of the scheduled time, the AI must override all preferences and trigger a loud emergency alarm.
